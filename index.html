Step-by-Step Umrah Guide

Prepare yourself spiritually and practically for every step of the sacred journey.

1. Intention (Niyyah)

Make the sincere intention to perform Umrah for the sake of Allah alone.

2. Wearing Ihram

Enter the state of Ihram from the Miqat and recite the Talbiyah.

3. Tawaf

Perform 7 circuits around the Holy Kaaba in a counter-clockwise direction.

4. Sa’i

Walk between the hills of Safa and Marwah 7 times as performed by Hajar (RA).

5. Hair Cutting

Men shave their head or cut hair short; women trim a small portion.

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for Umrah?

Passport (valid 6+ months), CNIC copy, 4 passport-size photos, and vaccination certificate.

What is the minimum age to perform Umrah?

There is no specific minimum age, but children under 18 must travel with guardians.

How long does the visa process take?

Typically 7–10 working days after complete document submission.

Can women perform Umrah alone?

Yes, women above 45 may perform Umrah without a Mahram with an approved group.

What types of accommodation are provided?

We offer 3-star, 4-star, and 5-star hotels within close proximity to Haram in Makkah and Madinah.

Are meals and transport included in the packages?

Yes, most of our packages include daily meals and air-conditioned transport for Ziyarat and local transfers.
